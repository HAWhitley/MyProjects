1. In order to construct the LAN using the one switch, here are the steps we did in order.

  1. We got the router and plugged in an ethernet cable into the wall and in the router.
  2. Next, we went to the router's configuration page and changed the IP address to 192.168.110.1
  3. We then plugged in our ethernet cables into ports 1 and 2 of the router and connected them to our computers
  4. We turned off the Wifi to get the wired connection so there were no problems.
  5. We then reset the router and both connected to the ethernet.
  6. Once we were connected, we both checked the status of the wired connection and made sure that the IP addresses were matching.
  7. Finally, before we tested the file sharing, we went to "other locations" to make sure we were connected.
  8. We used ethernet network protocol with physical data lines.
